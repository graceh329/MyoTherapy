Flip Hands

Instructions:

Start by holding your arm straight out in front of you with your palm facing up.
When instructed, turn your arm as much as far as you can so that your palm is facing down.
The progress bar indicates how much time you have left to complete the motion.
You will repeat this process 3 times.
